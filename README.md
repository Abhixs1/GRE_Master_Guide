# 📘 GRE General Test Structure

The **Graduate Record Examination (GRE) General Test** evaluates the skills required for success in graduate and business school programs. The exam measures **analytical writing, quantitative reasoning, and verbal reasoning** through an adaptive, section-level testing format.

---

## 📋 Exam Overview

| Section | Questions | Duration |
|:--------|:---------:|:--------:|
| ✍️ Analytical Writing | 1 Issue Essay | 30 Minutes |
| 🔢 Quantitative Reasoning – Section 1 | 12 | 18 Minutes |
| 🔢 Quantitative Reasoning – Section 2 | 15 | 26 Minutes |
| 📖 Verbal Reasoning – Section 1 | 12 | 21 Minutes |
| 📖 Verbal Reasoning – Section 2 | 15 | 23 Minutes |
| **Total** | **55 Questions + 1 Essay** | **1 Hour 58 Minutes** |

> **Section-Level Adaptive Test**
>
> The difficulty of the second **Quantitative** and **Verbal** sections is determined by your performance in the corresponding first section.

---

# ✍️ Analytical Writing

The Analytical Writing section measures your ability to think critically, construct logical arguments, and communicate ideas effectively.

### Exam Details

| Task | Duration |
|:-----|:--------:|
| Analyze an Issue | 30 Minutes |

### Skills Assessed

- Critical Thinking
- Logical Reasoning
- Argument Development
- Organization of Ideas
- Written Communication
- Grammar & Language Usage

---

# 🔢 Quantitative Reasoning

The Quantitative Reasoning section evaluates your mathematical reasoning, problem-solving ability, and data interpretation skills. The questions focus on applying mathematical concepts rather than memorizing formulas.

## Section Structure

| Section | Questions | Duration |
|:--------|:---------:|:--------:|
| Section 1 | 12 | 18 Minutes |
| Section 2 | 15 | 26 Minutes |

### Topics Covered

#### Arithmetic
- Integers
- Fractions & Decimals
- Ratios & Proportions
- Percentages
- Number Properties

#### Algebra
- Linear Equations
- Quadratic Equations
- Inequalities
- Functions
- Exponents & Roots

#### Geometry
- Lines & Angles
- Triangles
- Circles
- Polygons
- Coordinate Geometry
- Three-Dimensional Geometry

#### Data Analysis
- Statistics
- Probability
- Permutations & Combinations
- Data Interpretation
- Graphs & Charts

### Question Formats

- Multiple Choice (Single Answer)
- Multiple Choice (Multiple Answers)
- Numeric Entry
- Quantitative Comparison

---

# 📖 Verbal Reasoning

The Verbal Reasoning section assesses your ability to analyze written material, understand relationships between ideas, evaluate arguments, and interpret vocabulary within context.

## Section Structure

| Section | Questions | Duration |
|:--------|:---------:|:--------:|
| Section 1 | 12 | 21 Minutes |
| Section 2 | 15 | 23 Minutes |

### Question Types

#### Reading Comprehension
- Single-Paragraph Passages
- Multi-Paragraph Passages
- Critical Reasoning

#### Text Completion
- One Blank
- Two Blank
- Three Blank

#### Sentence Equivalence
Select **two** answer choices that produce sentences with equivalent meanings.

### Skills Assessed

- Vocabulary in Context
- Reading Comprehension
- Critical Reasoning
- Inference
- Tone & Author's Purpose

# 📊 GRE Scoring

| Measure | Score Range |
|:--------|:-----------:|
| Verbal Reasoning | **130 – 170** |
| Quantitative Reasoning | **130 – 170** |
| Analytical Writing | **0 – 6** |

**Maximum GRE Score:** **340 + Analytical Writing Score**

---

# 💡 Key Takeaways

- ✅ Section-level adaptive exam
- ✅ 55 questions and 1 analytical writing task
- ✅ Total testing time: **1 hour 58 minutes**
- ✅ No penalty for incorrect answers
- ✅ Calculator provided for Quantitative Reasoning
- ✅ Official score range: **260–340**, plus an Analytical Writing score of **0–6**
