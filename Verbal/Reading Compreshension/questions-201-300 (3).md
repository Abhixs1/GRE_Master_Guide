## Passage 21 — History of Technology

> The stirrup has been credited with reshaping medieval society: by letting a mounted warrior strike without being unhorsed, it is said to have made the armored knight, and with him feudalism itself. The stirrup made heavy cavalry possible, but possibility is not destiny, and treating it as such turns history into a machine.
